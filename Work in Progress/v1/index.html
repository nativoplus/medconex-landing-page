About Our App

Helping people adhere to prescribed medications using scientific methods.

We believe that patients are not alone in their health journey. When your loved one has a condition, it is natural to want to support them in the challenges that chronic diseases present to them. By providing a platform for patients to communicate their data, share their decisions and feelings about those prescriptions, between the people that care about their health outcomes, We aim to increase people's adherence to medications.

Special Features

With a vast set of features created with the purpose of facilitating your adherence to medications,

  • Easy to navigate dashboard

    With everything at your finger tips our dashboard makes it easier for you to access all the content in our application from a single page.

  • A good way to remember

    Great reminders feature integrated with the whole app

  • An integrated calendar for all your needs

    A calendar that shows all your upcoming medications, appointments, refills and much more.

    With our refill feature never run out of medications because you can always refill your prescriptions in just a few steps.
